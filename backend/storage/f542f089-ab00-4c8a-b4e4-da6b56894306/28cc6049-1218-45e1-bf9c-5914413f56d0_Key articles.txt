Inquiry:
In heart failure patients transitioning from step-down units, what is the effect of nurse-led discharge education on reducing readmission rates compared with standard discharge planning without structured nurse-led education. By addressing the self-management deficits identified in this population, this inquiry seeks to determine if nurse-directed behavioural coaching fosters the clinical stability necessary to prevent re-hospitalization
